About

Hi. I'm Jim, a London-based self-taught front-end developer specialising in JavaScript and React.

Improvements in technology over the last 25 years have been huge. We interact with technology on a daily basis and the tech industry now has an incredibly significant impact on the world. The industry is constantly changing and I believe that change is good, and that learning should never stop. I love learning new things on a daily basis as a developer and being at the forefront of this phenomenal industry.

I relish a good challenge and trying new things to solve them. I really enjoy the creative process of envisioning solutions to problems and developing products.

Having travelled extensively through more than 35 countries, including a recent 5 month spell living in Tbilisi, Georgia, I also have a global awareness and understanding of other cultures.
